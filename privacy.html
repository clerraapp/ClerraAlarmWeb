Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

At ClerraAlarm, we value your privacy. This policy explains how we collect and handle your information when you use our mobile application and website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary for the app to function:

  • Usage Data: Information about which missions you complete and your wake-up streaks.
  • Local Data: Alarms and mission settings are stored locally on your device.
  • Device Info: Operating system version and device model for performance monitoring.

2. How We Use Data

We use your data to:

  • Provided personalized mission challenges.
  • Track your "Morning Discipline" progress and streaks.
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs.

3. Third-Party Services

We use standard analytics tools (like Firebase or Sentry) to monitor app stability. These services may collect anonymous data about crashes or usage patterns.

4. Security

Your alarm data and photos (from photo missions) are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers unless explicitly stated by a feature you opt into.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via in-app notifications.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at Support.clerra@gmail.com.
