Support Center

My alarm didn't ring. What should I do?

Ensure that "Do Not Disturb" is configured to allow ClerraAlarm notifications, and that your device is not in silent mode. For best results, keep the app in the background.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage your subscription directly from your iPhone (App Store > Subscriptions) or Android (Play Store > Subscriptions) settings.

The Math mission is too hard!

You can adjust the difficulty level of each mission type in the Alarm Settings screen before setting your alarm.
