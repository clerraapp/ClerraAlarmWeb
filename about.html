Our Mission: Conquer the Morning.

ClerraAlarm was born out of a simple realization: the first ten minutes of your day determine the next sixteen hours. For most of us, those ten minutes are a blur of snoozing, half-sleep, and reactive scrolling.

The Struggle is Real

Willpower is at its lowest the moment we wake up. We built ClerraAlarm to provide the "external willpower" you need to transition from sleep to action without the struggle.

Discipline Through Design

We don't believe in waking up to loud, jarring noises. We believe in waking up to beauty, followed by intentional challenge. Our cinematic wallpapers and science-backed missions—Math, Physical, and Creative—are designed to spark your brain and body into gear.

Meet the Maker

ClerraAlarm is crafted for high-performers, students, and anyone who wants to reclaim their time. We are a small team dedicated to building tools that foster discipline and mental clarity.
