Refund Policy

At ClerraAlarm, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. However, because subscriptions are managed by third-party app stores, we follow their specific refund processes.

1. App Store Refunds (iOS)

If you purchased your subscription through the Apple App Store, all refund requests are handled by Apple. You can request a refund by visiting reportaproblem.apple.com.

2. Google Play Store Refunds (Android)

For purchases made on Android, you can request a refund through the Google Play Store. Usually, if you request a refund within 48 hours of purchase, Google may grant it directly.

3. 3-Day Free Trial

ClerraAlarm offers a 3-day free trial for new users. To avoid being charged, you must cancel the trial at least 24 hours before it expires.

4. Contact Us

If you encounter technical issues that prevent you from using the app, please reach out to our support team first at Support.clerra@gmail.com so we can help resolve the problem.
